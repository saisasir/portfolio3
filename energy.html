The ball briefly comes to a stop at the top of its swing too! So why does it have energy then?

That's because energy doesn't always depend on motion; it can be stored. Stored energy is called potential energy.

Gravitational potential energy is the energy an object possesses due to its relative position in a gravitational force field. In other words, if it can fall, it has potential energy.
